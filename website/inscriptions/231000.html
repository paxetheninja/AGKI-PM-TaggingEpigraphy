This inscription (IG IIÂ² 848) is a composite document from 222/1 BC containing two decrees passed by the Athenian state honoring the prytaneis of the tribe Leontis. The first decree, passed by the Demos, recognizes the prytaneis for their religious sacrifices to Apollo Prostaterios, Artemis Boulaia, and other traditional gods, as well as their administrative diligence.
