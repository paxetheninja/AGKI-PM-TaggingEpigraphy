AI Analysis Summary

This is a complex Athenian prytany decree from the archonship of Archelaus (222/1 BC). It follows the standard two-part structure: a decree of the Demos honoring the prytaneis for their religious sacrifices to Apollo and Artemis, and a decree of the Boule honoring specific officials (treasurer, secretary, priest, herald). The inscription concludes with a register of the fifty councilors of the tribe Leontis, categorized by their demes. The text is heavily reconstructed from fragments.
