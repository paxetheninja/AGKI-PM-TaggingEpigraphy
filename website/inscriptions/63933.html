AI Analysis Summary

This inscription (IG 11:4.1056) is a significant Athenian decree found on Delos, dating to 160/59 BC. It honors King Pharnakes I of Pontus and his wife, Queen Nysa, for their financial support and diplomatic friendship toward Athens. The text details the repayment of debts, the awarding of golden crowns, and the erection of bronze statues in the sanctuary of Delos. It also specifies that these honors be announced at major Athenian festivals (Dionysia, Panathenaia, Eleusinia, Ptolemaia), illustrating the close ties between the Hellenistic monarchs and the city of Athens during the period of the Athenian cleruchy on Delos.
