AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an Athenian decree dated to the archonship of Niketes (332/1 BC). It honors the prominent politician and Atthidographer Phanodemos of Thymaitadai for his legislative contributions to the sanctuary of Amphiaraos at Oropos. Phanodemos is credited with organizing the penteteric festival and securing funds for sacrifices and the sanctuary's maintenance. The decree follows standard Athenian diplomatic protocols, including the award of a golden crown and the instruction to set up a stone stele in the sanctuary. The text provides significant insight into the administration of extra-territorial Athenian sanctuaries and the role of individual legislators in religious life.
