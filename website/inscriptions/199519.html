AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Hellenistic decree from the Cretan city of Malla responding to an embassy from Teos. It confirms the traditional friendship and kinship between the two cities, recognizes the asylia (inviolability) of Teos based on oracular authority from Delphi and Didyma, and grants the Teians isopoliteia (equal citizenship) and ateleia (tax exemption) in Malla. The text mentions the kosmoi (Cretan magistrates) and the assembly, and orders the decree to be set up in the local sanctuary of Zeus Monnitios.
