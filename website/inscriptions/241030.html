AI Analysis Summary

This is a standard Delphic manumission record from the late 2nd century BC. It follows the legal formula of a 'sale' to Apollo Pythios, ensuring the slave's freedom while imposing a 'paramone' clause requiring him to stay with his former owners until their death. The text is well-preserved but contains several lacunae restored by epigraphists. The list of witnesses includes priests, council members, and arbitrators (diaitetai).
