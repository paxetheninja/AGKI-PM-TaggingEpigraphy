AI Analysis Summary

This is a classic Athenian prytany decree from 173/2 BC, during the archonship of Alexidos. It honors the prytaneis of the tribe Attalis for their successful administration and performance of religious duties, including sacrifices to Apollo, Artemis, and Phosphoros. The inscription includes two separate decrees: one by the Demos honoring the prytaneis as a whole, and another by the Council (Boule) honoring specific officials such as the treasurer, secretary, and priest. It concludes with a detailed register of the prytaneis listed by their demes.
