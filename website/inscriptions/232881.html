AI Analysis Summary

This is an Athenian citizenship decree dated to 319/8 BC during the archonship of Apollodoros. It honors Ainetos, son of Damon, for his military service and conduct during the war in Asia alongside King Alexander the Great. The decree grants him Athenian citizenship, including the right to enroll in a tribe, deme, and phratry, and awards him a gold crown worth 500 drachmae for his virtue and goodwill toward the Athenian people.
