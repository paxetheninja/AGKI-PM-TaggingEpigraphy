AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a naval catalogue from Lindos, Rhodes, dating to the mid-3rd century BC (ca. 265-260 BC). It commemorates a military expedition against 'tyrants' (likely during the Chremonidean War or the Second Syrian War). The text lists the commanding nauarchos (Nikasilos), trierarchs (Agathostratos and Gorgon), and the crew members who served on triemioliai (a specific type of Rhodian warship). The expedition was honored by the Rhodian people (demos) with crowns, and a dedication was made to Athena Lindia using the spoils (laphyra). The list includes citizens and at least one foreigner, Phormion of Halicarnassus, and uses the 'matros dexinas' formula for certain individuals, which is a specific Rhodian prosopographical feature.
