AI Analysis Summary

This document represents the administrative records of the Delian hieropoioi. It is divided into two primary functional sections: a set of financial accounts detailing expenditures for the physical maintenance and repair of the sanctuary of Apollo (specifically the Pythion), and a detailed inventory of votive offerings. The inventory focuses on golden crowns dedicated by prominent Hellenistic figures such as Ptolemy, Demetrius, and Nikokreon, as well as the Delian demos, providing precise weights for each object.
