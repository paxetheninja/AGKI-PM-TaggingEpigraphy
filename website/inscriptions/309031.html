This inscription is a decree issued by the Athenian soldiers stationed at the garrison of Rhamnous. It honors Antisthenes of Diomeia for his service as the military treasurer (tamias stratiotikon) during the archonship of Hagnias (216/215 BC). The text highlights his efficiency in distributing monthly wages (opsion) to the troops and his personal financial contributions to the city and the garrison. It follows the standard Attic decree format, concluding with instructions for the erection of a stone stele and the names of the officials responsible for the oversight.
