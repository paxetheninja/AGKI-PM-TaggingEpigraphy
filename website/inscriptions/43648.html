AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a detailed honorific decree from the city of Amphissa for a foreign physician, Menophantus of Hyrcania. It describes his medical skill (philotechnos), his dedication to saving those in danger, and his orderly conduct (eutakton kai sophrona) during his stay. The decree grants him proxeny, isopolity, the right to own property, and security in both war and peace. It also mentions the dispatch of copies of the decree to Scarpheia and Opus to ensure the honors are recognized elsewhere.
