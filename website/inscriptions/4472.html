AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic Athenian ephebic catalogue dating to the Severan period (c. 230-235 AD). It lists the names of the ephebes and the various officials overseeing their training, such as the polemarch, thesmothetai, sophronistai (moral overseers), and didaskaloi (teachers). The list is meticulously organized by the Athenian tribes (phylai), including Erechtheis, Aegeis, Pandionis, and others. The presence of names like 'Antinoeon' and 'Hadrianeon' reflects the Roman-era modifications to the Athenian civic structure.
