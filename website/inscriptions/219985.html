AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a royal ordinance (prostagma) from 41 BC issued by Cleopatra VII and her son Ptolemy XV Caesarion (Caesarion). It is an official letter addressed to the strategos of the Herakleopolite nome, intended to protect farmers in the Prosopite and Bubastite nomes from illegal tax exactions and administrative abuses. The decree mandates its own publication in both Greek and Egyptian (Demotic) scripts in prominent locations within the district.
