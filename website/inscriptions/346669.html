AI Analysis Summary

This is an Athenian honorific decree from 214/3 BC, during the archonship of Diocles. It honors the superintendents of the Mysteries for their successful management of the religious festivals at Eleusis and Agrae. The text details their performance of sacrifices to Demeter, Kore, and Iacchus, their provision of equipment at their own expense, and their administrative diligence in submitting financial accounts to the state auditors and the Metroon archive.
