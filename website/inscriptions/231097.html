AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a typical Athenian prytany decree from the mid-2nd century BCE. It honors the treasurer Philokrates and other officials for their diligent performance of sacrifices and administrative duties during their term. The text follows a standard formula: praise for the officials, the award of crowns, instructions for the publication of the decree on a stone stele in the Prytanikon, and a detailed list of the prytaneis (councilors) from the tribe Hippothontis, categorized by their demes. The mention of the 'treasurer of military funds' (tamias ton stratiotikon) for the cost of the stele is a common administrative detail of this period.
