AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal decree from the deme of Rhamnous honoring Dikaiarchos of Thria for his military and civic contributions during the reigns of Antigonus II Gonatas and Demetrius II. Dikaiarchos is praised for his command of the garrisons at Rhamnous, Eleusis, and Panakton, his protection of the Attic countryside during wartime, and his diplomatic intervention in Eretria to save Athenian citizens. He is also recognized for his financial support of the Nemesia festival. The decree orders the publication of the text on two stone stelae to be placed in the sanctuaries of Dionysus and Nemesis.
