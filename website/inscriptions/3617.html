AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragment of the Athenian treasury records, specifically an inventory of the Brauronion on the Acropolis. It dates to the early 4th century BC (c. 390/89 BC) and lists various precious metal objects (silver phialai, gold rings, ivory items) dedicated to Artemis Brauronia. The text meticulously records the weight of each item and the name of the donor, often including their patronymic and demotic. The mention of the archon Antigenes (407/6 BC) indicates that some of these items were dedicated or first recorded during the Peloponnesian War period.
