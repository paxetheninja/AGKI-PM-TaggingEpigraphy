AI Analysis Summary

This inscription, dated to 303 BCE, is a public decree from Athens recording a treaty of alliance (symmachia) with Sicyon. The text preserves the formal oath (horkos) invoking Zeus Agonios, Athena Areia, and Poseidon, which was standard for diplomatic agreements. It also contains honorific elements, specifically the awarding of a golden crown to the Sicyonian people, and administrative details regarding the publication of the treaty on a stone stele to be placed on the Acropolis. The mention of the demotic 'Cholargos' suggests the involvement of a citizen from that deme, likely as a proposer or official.
