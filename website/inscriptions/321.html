AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an Athenian temple inventory from the late 5th century BCE, specifically recording the transfer (paradosis) of sacred treasures of Athena between outgoing and incoming boards of treasurers (tamiai). It lists various precious metal objects, primarily silver phialai and cups, along with a gold crown, noting their weights in the Attic acrophonic numeral system. The text identifies officials by name, patronymic, and deme, following the standard administrative practices of the period.
