AI Analysis Summary

This inscription represents a series of victor lists (fasti agonistici) from the Great Asklepieia at Kos, dating to the first half of the 2nd century BC. The text is organized by year, naming the eponymous monarkhos and the agonothete, followed by the winners of musical contests (trumpeters, heralds, kitharists) and athletic events (pentathlon, stadion, dolichos, wrestling, boxing, pankration) divided into age groups (boys and men). The wide geographic range of the victors (from Miletos, Halikarnassos, Smyrna, etc.) underscores the panhellenic status of the Koan Asklepieia.
