AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an official letter from the High Priest of Asia, Marcus Ulpius Appuleius Eurycles, to the city of Aphrodisias. It deals with the administrative and financial arrangements for a musical contest (agon) established by the will of Flavius Lysimachus. The text includes specific financial figures for the endowment and coordinates the timing of the local games with the prestigious Barbilleia games in Ephesus, while also expressing loyalty to the Emperor Commodus. The text is fragmentary due to several lacunae, though the primary content remains legible.
