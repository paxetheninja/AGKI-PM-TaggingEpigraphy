AI Analysis Summary

This complex inscription preserves a series of decrees issued by the synedrion (council) of the league of cities associated with the sanctuary of Athena Ilias. It honors Malousios of Gargara for his extraordinary financial liturgy, which funded embassies to King Antigonus (likely Antigonus I Monophthalmus) regarding the freedom and autonomy of the cities, the construction of the theater at Ilion, and the support of the panegyris (festival). The text provides a detailed look at the intersection of private wealth, regional religious leagues, and Hellenistic royal diplomacy.
