This inscription is a classic example of the 'Inventories of the Treasurers of Athena and the Other Gods', specifically for the Pronaos of the Parthenon, dated to the archonship of Chabrias (417/6 BCE). The text follows a strict formula: naming the outgoing treasurers, the incoming treasurers, and the secretary, followed by a list of items (mostly silver phialai and drinking vessels) with their weights recorded in the acrophonic numeral system. The use of the stoichedon 61 grid is typical for this period of Athenian epigraphy.
