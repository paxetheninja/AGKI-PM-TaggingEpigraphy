AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree from Prusa ad Olympum, dating to the period of the Mithridatic Wars (c. 88-67 BC). It commemorates an anonymous local benefactor for his 'euergetism' (public service), specifically highlighting his diplomatic missions to Italy and his military leadership during a siege. The mention of King Mithridates VI and a Roman 'autokrator' (general) underscores the city's strategic and political involvement in the conflict between the Roman Republic and the Kingdom of Pontus. The text emphasizes the subject's use of private funds for public benefit and his bravery (andreia) in defending the city.
