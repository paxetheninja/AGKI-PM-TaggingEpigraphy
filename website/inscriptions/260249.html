AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Stratonikeia (likely from the sanctuary at Lagina) is a public notice (prosgramma) and a decree concerning the management and protection of the sacred grove of the goddess Hecate. It establishes strict rules against the introduction of livestock into the grove to prevent damage to the trees and plants. It also outlines the administrative duties of the annual priests and the eunuchs in maintaining the site, and specifies fines and procedures for those who violate the sacred laws, including the public sale of confiscated animals by the treasurers.
