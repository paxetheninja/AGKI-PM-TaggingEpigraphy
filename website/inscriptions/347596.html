This inscription is a prytany decree from Athens, dated to the archonship of Hippias (181/0 BC). It honors the prytaneis of the tribe Erechtheis for their pious performance of sacrifices to Apollo Prostaterios and Artemis Boulaia, and for their diligent administration of the Council and People's affairs. The decree follows the standard Athenian formula, including the archon's name, the secretary, the proposer, and the publication instructions. It concludes with a catalogue of the prytaneis arranged by deme. The text is fragmentary, with significant portions restored based on parallel inscriptions.
