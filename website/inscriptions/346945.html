AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Eleusis honors a prominent Hierophant from the deme of Paiania and the Eumolpidae clan. It serves as a record of his distinguished career, holding major Athenian offices such as Eponymous Archon, Strategos over the Hoplites, and Herald of the Areopagus. Crucially, it documents his role in the Eleusinian Mysteries, specifically his initiation of the Emperor Lucius Verus. The text also traces his elite lineage through several generations of Athenian officials and mentions his relationship with the Emperors Hadrian and Antoninus Pius.
