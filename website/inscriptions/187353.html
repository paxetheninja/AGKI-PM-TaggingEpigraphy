AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Iasos contains a decree from the people of Calymna honoring five Iasian judges (Cleandros, Leon, Cephalos, Theodoros, and another Leon). The judges were sent to mediate over 350 legal disputes, most of which they settled through persuasion, while the rest were decided by vote according to royal edicts and laws. In gratitude, Calymna awarded the judges golden crowns, citizenship, and the right to own land. The decree was to be inscribed and set up in the Temple of Zeus and Artemis, and announced at the Dionysia festival.
