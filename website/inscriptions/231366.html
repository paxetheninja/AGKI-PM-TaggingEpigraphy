AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a catalog of the Athenian Council (Boule) for the archonship of Pythodotus (343/2 BC). It lists the councilors by their ten tribes and respective demes. The heading indicates that the councilors, along with the treasurers and secretaries, were honored with crowns by the Athenian people for their conduct and justice during their year of office. The text is a significant administrative record from the Late Classical period, providing a snapshot of Athenian civic participation and the honorific culture of the city's democratic institutions.
