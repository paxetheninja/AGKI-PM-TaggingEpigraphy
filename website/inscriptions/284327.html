This inscription from Patara, Lycia, dates to the late 1st century BC or early 1st century AD. It is a detailed funerary monument record established by Publius Paconius Hermias. The presence of Roman names (Paconius, Claudius, Ofillia) reflects the Romanization of the Lycian elite during this period.
