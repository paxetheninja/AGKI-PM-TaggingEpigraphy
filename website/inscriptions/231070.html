AI Analysis Summary

This is a typical Athenian prytany decree from the 2nd century BC. It follows a rigid formula: dating by archon and secretary, recording the assembly meeting in Piraeus, the proposal by Lysandros, and the commendation of the outgoing prytaneis of the tribe Kekropis. The honors are granted for their successful performance of sacrifices to Apollo and Artemis, and for their general administrative efficiency. The text concludes with instructions for the publication of the decree on a stone stele.
