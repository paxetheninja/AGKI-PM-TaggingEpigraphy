AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a typical Athenian prytany decree from the Hellenistic period (175/4 BC). It honors the prytaneis of the tribe Kekropis for their religious and administrative diligence. The text follows a standard formula: dating by archon and prytany, naming the secretary and proposer, describing the sacrifices made to Apollo and Artemis, and concluding with the award of a golden crown and the instruction to erect a stone stele in the Prytanikon. The text is heavily restored but the formulas are well-attested in the Athenian epigraphic record.
