AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant Lindian decree from 22 AD concerning the 'parakatatheke' (sacred deposit/fund) of Athena Lindia and Zeus Polieus. It outlines administrative procedures for the sacred treasurers (hierotamiai) and the priest, including the sale of old bronze and iron sacred objects to generate funds. It also addresses the need to provide inscriptions for previously uninscribed statues in the sanctuary. The latter part of the text contains a subscription list (epidosis) where citizens and officials pledged money to the goddess's fund. The text is inscribed on Lartian stone, a local Rhodian limestone.
