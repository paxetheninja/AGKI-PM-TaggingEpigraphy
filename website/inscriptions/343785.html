AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a citizenship decree from Samos, dating to the period shortly after 306 BC, as evidenced by the mention of the tribe Demetrieis (named after Demetrius Poliorcetes). It honors Pythokles and Kallinikos, sons of Pythodoros from Arge (likely in the Samian Peraia), for their assistance to Samian exiles during the period of the cleruchy and their continued support after the restoration of the Samian state. The decree grants them citizenship, rights of safe passage (esplous and ekplous), and orders the publication of the text on a stone stele in the sanctuary of Hera. The text is fragmentary, requiring significant restoration in the opening and closing sections.
