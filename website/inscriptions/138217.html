This inscription (IG XI,4 1053) is a diplomatic letter from the city of Thessaloniki to Delos, dated to approximately 230 BC. It acknowledges the arrival of the Delian envoy Boulon and the receipt of decrees honoring Admetos, son of Bokros. The text is significant for documenting the interstate relations between Macedonia and the Cyclades during the Hellenistic period.
