This inscription is a fragment of the Athenian Naval Inventories (tabulae curatorum navalium), likely dating to the mid-4th century BC. It catalogs the equipment of the Athenian fleet, specifically mentioning oars (thalamiai, zygiai, perineo), masts (histos), and rudders (pedalia). It lists the names of trierarchs and their demes, recording who is in possession of specific items or who owes them back to the state.
