AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragment of a temple inventory from Delos, dated to approximately 190 BC by the mention of the archon Telemnestos. It records the audit of the sacred treasures of Apollo, including numerous gold and silver vessels (phialai, skyphoi) and crowns. A significant portion of the text lists dedications made by the Antigonid royal family (Demetrios Poliorcetes, Phila, Stratonike, and Antigonos Gonatas), which were maintained in the temple treasury for generations. The text also mentions local officials (hieropoioi) and delegations (theoroi) from other cities like Keos.
