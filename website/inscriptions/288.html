AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is part of the Athenian Tribute Quota Lists (ATL), specifically List 20, dating to the archonship of Euthynos (435/4 BCE). It records the 1/60th share (aparchÄ“) of the tribute paid by the members of the Delian League, which was dedicated to the treasury of Athena Polias. The text is organized by geographical districts: the Islands (Nesiotikos), the Hellespont (Hellespontios), and Thrace (Thrakios). It lists the names of the contributing cities and the amounts paid, though many of the numerical values are fragmentary or represented by placeholders in this transcription.
