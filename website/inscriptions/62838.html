This inscription is a comprehensive financial account of the hieropoioi of Delos for the year 207 BC (IG XI,2 199). It documents the complex administration of the Sanctuary of Apollo, including the maintenance of public buildings (Neorion, Stoa by the Posideion, Aphrodision) under the supervision of the architect Gorgos. It details the procurement of timber, the inventory of sacred treasures (vessels, crowns, and dedications) transferred between officials, the leasing of sacred houses and agricultural estates (including those on Mykonos), and a list of individuals owing interest on loans from the sacred fund. The text provides invaluable evidence for Hellenistic economic history, temple administration, and architectural terminology.
