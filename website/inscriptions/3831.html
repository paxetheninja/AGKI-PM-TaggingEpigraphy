AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragment of the Athenian Naval Inventories (Tabulae Curatorum Navalium), specifically dating to the early 4th century BCE (before 370/69 BCE). It records the accountability of trierarchs for the naval gear (skeue) of triremes, including both wooden (xylina) and hanging (kremasta) equipment. The text mentions the overseers of the dockyards (epimeletai ton neorion) and the storehouse (skeuotheke). It is a primary source for Athenian naval administration and prosopography, featuring prominent figures like the generals Chabrias and Timotheos.
