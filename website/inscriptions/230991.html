AI Analysis Summary

This is a standard Athenian prytany decree from the archonship of Leochares (228/7 BC). It records the honors granted to the prytaneis of the tribe Kekropis for their successful administration and their performance of sacrifices to Apollo Prostaterios and Artemis Boulaia. The text follows the typical formulaic structure of Hellenistic Athenian decrees, including dating by archon and prytany, the naming of officials, the record of the assembly meeting in the theater, and instructions for the publication of the decree on a stone stele in the Prytanikon.
