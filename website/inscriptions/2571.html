AI Analysis Summary

This is a formal Athenian decree from the archonship of Euthykritos (328/7 BCE). It honors Dendrokles, the priest of Asclepius, for his diligent and pious management of the sanctuary and the theater. The text includes typical administrative formulas regarding the prytany, the secretary, and the funding for the monument's creation and the priest's reward, which includes a golden crown worth 500 drachmae.
