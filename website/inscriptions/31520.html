AI Analysis Summary

This is a civic decree from the city of Geronthrai, likely dating to the 2nd century BC. It honors individuals from Carystus and Gytheion by granting them the status of proxenoi and benefactors. The decree includes standard privileges such as the right to own land and houses (enktesis), tax immunity (ateleia), and safety in war and peace. It was to be set up in the sanctuary of Apollo by the ephors during the term of the strategos Xenophanes.
