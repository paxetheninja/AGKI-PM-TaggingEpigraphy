AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a detailed circus curse (defixio) likely from Carthage, dating to the 2nd or 3rd century AD. It targets the Green faction (Prasinoi), specifically naming several charioteers and calling upon a spirit of the dead (nekydaimon) and various magical names (voces magicae) to bind the horses and drivers. The curse seeks to physically disable the competitors, preventing them from winning, turning the posts (kampteres), or even leaving the starting gates (hippaphesis). The text mentions depositing the curse in sea-shells (ostrakois thalassiois), a specific ritual practice.
