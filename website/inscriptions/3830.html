AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is part of the 'Tabulae Curatorum Navalium' (naval inventories) of Athens, dated to 373/2 BCE. It records the state of equipment for various triremes (e.g., Panthera, Palaia Epiklerotos). The text lists specific quantities of oars (thranite, zygian, thalamian), rudders (pedalia), and ladders (klimakides). It identifies the trierarchs responsible for these items by name and demotic, reflecting the administrative oversight of the Athenian navy during the Second Athenian League.
