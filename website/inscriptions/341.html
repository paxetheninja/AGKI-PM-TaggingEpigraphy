AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic example of the Athenian 'Traditiones' (handover records) of the Treasurers of Athena. Dated to 417/6 BCE, it records the transfer of sacred items kept in the Hekatompedon (the cella of the Parthenon). The text follows a strict formula: naming the incoming and outgoing boards of treasurers and their secretaries, followed by an itemized list of precious metal objects (votives) with their weights recorded in the acrophonic numeral system.
