AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragment of the financial accounts of the sanctuary of Apollo on Delos, dating to approximately 172 BC. It documents the administrative and fiscal activities of the Hieropoioi and Tamiai, including the collection of interest from loans, rents from sacred properties, and the repayment of debts. The text mentions specific legal contracts (syngraphai) and provides a prosopography of Delian officials during the archonships of Parmenon and Polyxenus.
