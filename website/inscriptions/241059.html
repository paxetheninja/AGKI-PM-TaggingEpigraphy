AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal manumission record from Delphi, dated to the late 2nd century AD. It follows the standard Delphic formula where a slave is sold to Pythian Apollo to secure their freedom. The text includes a dating formula by the local archon, a list of council members, the terms of the sale (3 minae), the legal status of the freedwoman (Sosila), and a list of witnesses including priests and private citizens. It also notes the deposition of the record in the city's public archives via the secretary Tiberius Julius Lycarion.
