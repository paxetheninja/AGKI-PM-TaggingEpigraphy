AI Analysis Summary

This is an honorific decree from Kalymna dating to the late 3rd century BC (c. 227 BC), honoring Theukrates, son of Nasigenes. The text records his benefactions, including financial support for soldiers and contributions related to King Antigonus. It grants him citizenship, specifying his assignment to a tribe and other civic divisions (triakas and pentekoste). The decree was to be inscribed on a stele and placed in the Sanctuary of Apollo.
