AI Analysis Summary

This is an Athenian decree from the archonship of Theodotus (387/6 BCE). It concerns the administrative and legal status of Athenian cleruchs (settlers) on the island of Lemnos. The text outlines religious duties (sacrifices to the Twelve Gods), financial regulations (leasing of confiscated property), and legal procedures for disputes involving the settlers. It references the treatment of settlers on Salamis as a precedent and specifies that the decree be erected on the Acropolis.
