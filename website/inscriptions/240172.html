This inscription is a proxeny decree from Delphi honoring Aristonoos, son of Sosandros, of Patara. Aristonoos is a known historical figure, a poet who composed hymns to Apollo. The decree recognizes his piety (eusebeia) and his helpfulness to both the sanctuary and the citizens of Delphi. It grants him and his descendants the status of proxenos, along with privileges such as priority in consulting the oracle (promanteia), priority in legal trials (prodikia), and the right to own land and house in Delphi (enktesis). The text also includes administrative instructions for its publication in the sanctuary of Apollo and the notification of the Ainianes.
