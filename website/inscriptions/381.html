AI Analysis Summary

This inscription, known as the 'Logistai Inscription' (IG I³ 369), is a crucial document for understanding Athenian public finance during the Archidamian War. It records the audit of loans made from the sacred treasuries of Athena Polias, Athena Nike, and the 'Other Gods' to the Athenian state for military expenditures between 426/5 and 423/2 BCE. It provides precise details on the principal sums, the interest accrued, and the specific generals and campaigns (such as those in the Peloponnese and Samos) for which the funds were used.
