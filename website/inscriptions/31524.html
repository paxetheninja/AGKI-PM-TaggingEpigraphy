AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant fragment of the Edict of Diocletian on Maximum Prices (301 AD) found in Geronthrai. It contains several chapters of the edict, specifically those dealing with the wages of teachers and legal professionals, the prices of various types of leather and hides, footwear (including military and civilian styles), wooden tools and vehicles, and a very detailed section on linen textiles from famous production centers like Tarsus, Laodicea, and Scythopolis. The text reflects the complex economic administration of the Tetrarchy.
