AI Analysis Summary

This is the monumental cult inscription (Nomos) of King Antiochos I of Kommagene from the sanctuary at Nemrud Dağ. It is a foundational text for the ruler cult of the Kommagenean dynasty, blending Persian and Greek religious traditions. The text details the king's lineage, his piety, the construction of the hierothesion, and the legal and ritual requirements for the maintenance of his cult, including the appointment of priests and the celebration of his birthday and coronation day. It concludes with a series of divine sanctions against anyone who would dare to alter or damage the sanctuary.
