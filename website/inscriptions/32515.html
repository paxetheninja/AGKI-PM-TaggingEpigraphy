AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a diplomatic dossier from the mid-2nd century BC, documenting the interaction between the Magnesian League and the Arcadian city of Kletor. It consists of a covering letter from the Magnesian strategos Zoilos and the secretary Meidias, followed by a formal decree (psephisma) passed by the Magnesian council (synedroi) and assembly (ekklesia). The documents honor foreign judges (dikastai), specifically Dorotheos and Asklepiades, who were sent to Kletor to resolve legal disputes. The decree grants them the status of proxenoi and euergetai (benefactors), along with privileges like ateleia (tax exemption) and asylia (inviolability). The text mandates the publication of these honors on stone stelae in prominent religious and civic spaces: the sanctuary of Artemis Iolkia in Demetrias and the agora of Kletor.
