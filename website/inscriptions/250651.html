AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Ephesus is a late antique ecclesiastical ordinance or official letter issued by Hypatios. It begins with a theological reflection on the humility and passion of Jesus Christ, referencing the 'divine apostle' (Paul). The text transitions into practical regulations for the Church of the Virgin Mary (Theotokos) in Ephesus, specifically addressing the 'dekanoi' (deacons) and 'kanonikai' (canonesses). It strictly forbids charging money for the burial of the dead, characterizing such greed as impiety and threatening those who accept payment or neglect the poor with excommunication from the holy churches.
