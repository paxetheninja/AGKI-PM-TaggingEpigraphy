The inscription (I.Kallatis 45) is a detailed decree from a religious association (thiasos) in Kallatis concerning the construction of a temple for Dionysos. It establishes the administrative process for managing funds through three elected members and provides a subscription list where members contribute either money or labor (ergatas).
