AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree issued by the Magnesian League (synedroi) in the 1st century BC. It honors Sopatros, son of Euboulos, for his distinguished service as strategos. The text details his civic contributions, specifically his donation of 4,000 denarii to the grain fund (sitonikon) during a grain shortage (sitodeia) and his management of a crisis involving a plague (loimos). The decree concludes with the decision to set up a painted portrait (eikona grapten) of him. The mention of denarii and a Roman proconsul (anthypatos) reflects the Roman administrative influence in Thessaly during this period.
