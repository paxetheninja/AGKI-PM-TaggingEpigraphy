This is an Athenian ephebic decree dating to the mid-1st century BCE (archonship of Polycharmos). It honors the ephebes and their supervisor (kosmetes) for their religious devotion, military training (including a voyage to Salamis), and intellectual contributions, specifically the donation of books to the library in the Ptolemaion. The text provides significant insight into the late Hellenistic ephebeia, emphasizing both physical training and cultural education.
