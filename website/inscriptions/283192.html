AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a subscription list (epidosis) from the sanctuary of Leto (Letoion) near Xanthos. It dates to the 2nd century BC and records contributions from citizens for the 'chrysosis' (gilding) of the cult statues of the Letoid triad: Apollo, Artemis, and Leto, as well as the Parthenos (Athena). The preamble emphasizes the donors' piety (eusebeia) and their honorable disposition toward the people (demos). The list includes many names typical of the Lycian region, some showing Greek influence and others indigenous or Persian-influenced onomastics. The text is dated by the priesthood of Androbios and the month of Audnaios.
