AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal decree from the Hellenistic period issued by the Guild of the Artists of Dionysus in Ptolemais Hermiou, Egypt. It honors Lysimachus, a high-ranking official, for his support of the guild and his loyalty to King Ptolemy II Philadelphus and the royal family. The text is significant for its detailed list of guild members, providing a rare glimpse into the organization and professional diversity of theatrical and musical performers in Ptolemaic Egypt, including poets, actors, musicians, and support staff.
