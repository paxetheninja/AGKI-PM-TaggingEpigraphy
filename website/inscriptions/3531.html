AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a late 3rd-century BC decree from Attica, issued by a religious association (orgeones). It honors Agathon of Phlya and his wife Zeuxion for their pious and diligent service as priest and priestess. The text highlights their financial integrity, their management of the association's resources, and their personal dedications to the gods. As a reward, they are granted praise and golden crowns. The decree concludes with instructions for its publication on a stone stele to be placed in the sanctuary.
