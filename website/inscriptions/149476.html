AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree from the city of Beroea for a citizen named Harpalos. It highlights his noble lineage, his service as a priest of the 'Benefactor Gods' (Theoi Euergetai), his diplomatic embassies on behalf of the city, and his financial generosity in maintaining public monuments. The decree grants him a crown and a bronze statue, and mandates that the text be recorded on a stone stele to be placed near the statue. The text is fragmentary with significant gaps in the middle section.
