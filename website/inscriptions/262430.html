This inscription is a decree (psephisma) issued by a 'syngeneia' (a kinship-based association) at the Sanctuary of Sinuri, near Mylasa in Caria. It concerns the financial management of a 200-drachma fund and the leasing (misthosis) of property. The text outlines the responsibilities of the treasurers (tamiai) and the penalties for non-payment of interest (tokos). It is dated by the eponymous stephanephoros Hermias. The instruction to inscribe the text on the 'parastas' of the gateway indicates its public and permanent legal nature within the sanctuary space.
