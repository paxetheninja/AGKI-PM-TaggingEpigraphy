AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant example of a 'symbolon' or judicial treaty, likely between Stymphalos and another Peloponnesian city. It provides a detailed legal framework for handling cross-border disputes, focusing on the prosecution of perjury (false testimony), the appointment of reconcilers (synlytai), and the specific roles of magistrates like the demiurges, generals (stratagoi), and archons. The text also mentions the use of Aeginetan drachmas for fines and the requirement to publish the agreement on stelae.
