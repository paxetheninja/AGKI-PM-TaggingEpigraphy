AI Analysis Summary

The inscription is a significant record of interstate arbitration in the Hellenistic period. It details the resolution of grievances (egklemata) between Temnos and Clazomenae following a war. The Knidian judges emphasize the restoration of 'friendship and goodwill' (philia kai eunoia) and declare an amnesty (amnestia) for wartime actions. A specific part of the dispute concerns the boundaries of a sacred precinct, where one party was accused of moving boundary stelae and encroaching on land dedicated to the gods.
