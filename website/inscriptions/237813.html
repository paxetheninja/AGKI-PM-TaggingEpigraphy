This is an Athenian decree from the archonship of Chairondas (338/7 BC), immediately following the Battle of Chaeronea. It honors two Akarnanians, Phormion and Karphinas, granting them citizenship (indicated by the right to choose a tribe and deme) and confirming previous gifts. The decree recognizes their military assistance ('fought alongside with a force') to Athens. The text is inscribed on a stone stele set up on the Acropolis. The text is fragmentary, requiring restoration of standard formulas and names based on the stoichedon pattern.
