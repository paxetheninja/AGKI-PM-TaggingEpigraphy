AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a typical Athenian prytany decree dating to the archonship of Eupolemos (169/8 BC). It records the honors bestowed upon the prytaneis of the tribe Antiochis for their successful administration and performance of sacrifices to Apollo Prostaterios and Artemis (Boulaia and Phosphoros). The text includes a detailed list of the prytaneis categorized by their demes, which is a vital source for Athenian prosopography and administrative history. The decree was to be set up in the Prytanikon, and the costs were covered by the military treasurer.
