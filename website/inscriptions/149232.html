AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant decree from the city of Phalanna in Perrhaibia, dating to the early 2nd century BC. It serves as a sacred law regulating the management and protection of the 'chora' (sacred land) belonging to Pluto and Persephone. The text addresses illegal construction within the sanctuary (temenos), mandates the demolition of unauthorized structures, and orders the publication of the decree on a stone column to ensure the boundaries are 'clear to all'. It also details the administrative process involving the tagoi (Thessalian magistrates) and the use of public funds for the monument's creation.
