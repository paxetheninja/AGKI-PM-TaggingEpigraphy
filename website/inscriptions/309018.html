AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal honorific decree from the deme of Rhamnous dating to the archonship of Niketes (225/224 BC). It honors Menandros, son of Teisandros of Eitea, for his exemplary service as a trierarch. The text details his financial generosity, including paying for ship repairs from his own funds, providing oil for the training of young men (neaniskoi), and performing sacrifices to Zeus Soter, Athena Soteira, and Nemesis for the welfare and concord of his crew. The decree concludes with instructions for its publication on a stone stele and the appointment of a committee (Timokrates, Archistratos, and Diokles) to oversee the expenses.
