AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from the Asklepieion of Kos is a significant sacred law (lex sacra) ratified as a public decree. It details the ritual requirements for the cult of Demeter, including purifications necessary after birth, death, or suicide, and the handling of sacred objects. The analysis has been corrected to identify 'Olympia' as an epithet of Demeter rather than a priestess. It also identifies the eponymous magistrate (monarchos), the board of Prostatai, and the proposer Exagetos.
