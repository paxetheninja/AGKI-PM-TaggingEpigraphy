AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Lagina is an honorific decree issued by the Demos and Boule to honor a family of priests. It details their lifelong service to Hekate and Artemis of Panamara, their financial generosity during festivals (including distributions to citizens, Romans, and residents), and their participation in the Dionysian mysteries. The mention of Marcus Agrippa provides a chronological and cultural anchor to the early Roman Imperial period.
