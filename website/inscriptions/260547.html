AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal decree from Magnesia on the Maeander. It acknowledges an embassy from the Alarbeni and honors Menogenes, the envoy, as well as a group of foreign judges (Philocrates, Socrates, and Aristagoras) and their secretary (Moschus). The decree outlines the publication process, including the creation of a white stone stele to be placed in the most prominent part of the agora, and the provision of hospitality (xenia) and an invitation to the Prytaneum for the honored individuals. It also details the financial responsibility of the city's treasurers (oikonomoi) for the costs of the monument.
