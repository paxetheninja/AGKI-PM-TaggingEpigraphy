AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a detailed decree from Magnesia on the Maeander concerning the annual festival of Zeus Sosipolis. It outlines the ritual selection of a bull at the start of the sowing season, its maintenance by the city, and its eventual sacrifice during the month of Artemision. The text is a primary source for understanding the intersection of civic administration and religious ritual in the Hellenistic period, involving a wide range of officials from military polemarchs to superintendents of children and women.
