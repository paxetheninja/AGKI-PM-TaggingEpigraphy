AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic example of an astragalos oracle (astragalomanteia) from Termessos in Pisidia. It consists of a series of verses that interpret the numerical outcomes of throwing dice (astragaloi). The text invokes Apollo and Hermes as patrons of the oracle and provides specific advice on matters such as commerce, travel, health, and general success. It specifically mentions the 'Chian' throw and other numerical combinations, concluding with an exhortation to revere Zeus the Great King.
