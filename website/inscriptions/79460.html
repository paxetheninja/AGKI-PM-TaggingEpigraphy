AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragmentary honorific decree from the city of Thasos. It honors the people of Smyrna and a specific judge, Demetrius son of Archeas, for their service in judicial matters. The text follows the standard formula for decrees concerning foreign judges, awarding golden crowns for virtue and justice and granting privileges such as front-row seating at games and access to the council and assembly. The honors were to be proclaimed during the Dionysia festival during the tragic contests.
