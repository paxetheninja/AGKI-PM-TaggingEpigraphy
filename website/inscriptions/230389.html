AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant decree issued by the city of Thyateira in Lydia, found in Athens and dated to the reign of Hadrian (after 132 AD). It records the city's gratitude for Hadrian's benefactions, including his establishment of the Panhellenion, his provision of grain (sitou), and financial support. The decree was intended to be displayed on the Athenian Acropolis to demonstrate Thyateira's participation in the Panhellenic league. It also provides prosopographical data regarding Ti. Claudius Menogenes Caecilianus, a prominent local elite who served as a priest of Dionysos and high priest of the Sebastoi.
