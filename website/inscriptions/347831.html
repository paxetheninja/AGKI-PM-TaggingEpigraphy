AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an Athenian honorary decree dating to 266/5 BC, during the archonship of Nicias of Otryne. It honors Strombichos, a soldier who had previously served under Demetrius but joined the Athenian cause during the struggle for the city's liberty. The text specifically mentions his participation in the siege of the Mouseion hill and his continued goodwill toward the Athenian people. The document provides significant administrative detail, including the names of the secretary, the proposer, and the presiding officers.
