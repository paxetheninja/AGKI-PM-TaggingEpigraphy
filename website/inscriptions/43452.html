AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a decree of the Acarnanian League (Koinon of the Acarnanians) from 207 BC, found at Magnesia on the Maeander. It records the official response to a Magnesian embassy (Aristodemos, Aristeas, and Antenor) seeking recognition for the panhellenic status of the festival of Artemis Leukophryene and the inviolability (asylia) of their city and territory. The Acarnanians grant these requests, establish thearodokoi in their constituent cities (Medion, Thyrreion, Anaktorion, etc.), and provide a financial contribution for the sacrifice. The decree was to be set up in the sanctuary of Apollo Aktios in Acarnania, while this copy was found in Magnesia.
