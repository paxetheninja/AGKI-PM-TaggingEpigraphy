AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Samian citizenship decree dating to the late 4th or early 3rd century BC. It honors Pythokles and Kallinikos, sons of Pythodoros, for their assistance to Samian exiles and their continued goodwill after the restoration of the Samian democracy. The decree grants them citizenship, the right to own property, and access to the council and assembly. It concludes with instructions for the secretary of the council to record the decree on a stone stele and set it up in the sanctuary of Hera (the Heraion).
