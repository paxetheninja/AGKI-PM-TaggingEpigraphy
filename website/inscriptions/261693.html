AI Analysis Summary

This inscription (IStr 15) is a formal decree from the city of Stratonikeia in Caria, dating to the Roman Imperial period (likely 1st or 2nd century AD). It honors Tiberius Flavius Menandros (son of Stratokles) and his wife Flavia Leontis for their extraordinary generosity and religious service. The couple served as high priests of the Imperial cult and stephanephoroi of Apollo. A primary focus of the decree is their funding of a bathhouse (balaneion) and their performance of the gymnasiarchy, which included providing oil for citizens and slaves. The decree also mandates that the name of Zeus Panamaros, the city's protector, be invoked in all official documents.
