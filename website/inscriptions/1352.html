AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic example of an Athenian public list from the late 5th century BCE. It is organized by tribes, specifically mentioning Pandionis and Leontis. The names are listed in columns, a format used for military casualty lists or rosters of officials such as the Boule or other civic bodies. The orthography (e.g., the use of 'XS' for Xi) confirms a pre-Euclidean Attic date. The presence of many fragmentary names and brackets indicates significant surface damage or loss of the original stone.
