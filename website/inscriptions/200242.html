🤖 AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant diplomatic dossier from Hierapytna, dating to the early 2nd century BC. It records a series of international agreements: a comprehensive military alliance with Rhodes that includes provisions for naval support, anti-piracy cooperation, and specific pay scales for mercenary or allied troops; a treaty with the Cretan city of Lyttos involving mutual defense and religious oaths; and a decree honoring envoys from Magnesia on the Maeander. The text provides rare insight into the logistical and financial details of Hellenistic military cooperation, such as the daily wages (obols and drachmae) for soldiers and officers.
