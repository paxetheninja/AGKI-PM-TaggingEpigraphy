AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a typical Athenian prytany list from the Roman period (c. 180 AD). It records the names of the councilors from the tribe Attalis who served during the archonship of Athenodoros. The list is structured by demes and includes the 'aisitoi', a group of permanent officials and religious figures (like the Hierophant and Dadouchos of the Eleusinian Mysteries) who were entitled to dine at the Prytaneion. The presence of Roman names (e.g., Marcius Severus) reflects the integration of Roman citizens into the Athenian civic structure during this era.
