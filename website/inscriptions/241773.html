AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal manumission record from Delphi, dated to the archonship of Kleodemos (c. 91 BC). It follows the standard Delphic formula where a slave (Hairesis) is sold to Apollo Pythios by her owner (Astoxenos) to achieve the status of 'eleuthera' (free). The document includes a detailed dating formula, the price paid (4.5 minae), conditions of freedom (the slave is to be free and 'anaphapton' - untouchable), and a list of witnesses including local priests and officials.
