AI Analysis Summary

This is a public decree from the city of Minoa on the island of Amorgos. It records the honors bestowed upon Abrytis, daughter of Agathopous, who died prematurely. The text highlights her noble lineage and the status of her husband, Philagathos, who held the office of dekaprotos. The city orders a public funeral and a posthumous golden crown to console her family. The Greek text contains several lacunae and reconstructed letters, particularly in the names of family members.
