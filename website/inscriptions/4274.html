AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic Athenian ephebic catalogue dating to the Hadrianic period (125/6 AD). It begins with the name of the kosmetes (Aelius Pontius Nymphodotos) and his assistants (hypokosmetai), followed by a list of gymnasiarchs organized by the months of the Attic calendar (Boedromion through Metageitnion). The lower portion contains a list of ephebes and instructors (paideutai). The text shows significant lacunae and fragmentary names, typical of large marble stelae from the Athenian Agora or Gymnasium.
