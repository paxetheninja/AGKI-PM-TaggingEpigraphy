The inscription is a standard and complete manumission record from Delphi, dated to the mid-2nd century BC. It follows the typical formula of a 'sale to the god' (Apollo Pythios), including the names of the archons for dating, the identification of the seller (Theoxenos of Amphissa), the slave (Straton of Apamea), the price (5 minae), and the conditions of paramone (remaining service).
