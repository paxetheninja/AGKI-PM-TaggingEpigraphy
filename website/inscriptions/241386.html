AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal manumission record from Delphi, dated by the archonship of Nikoboulos (c. 170-157 BC). It follows the 'sale to the god' formula where Stratonikos of Histiaea sells two female slaves, Kleuta and Straton, to Apollo Pythios for 4 silver minae to ensure their freedom. The text includes a paramone clause, stipulating that the women must continue to serve Stratonikos as long as he lives. It concludes with a list of witnesses, including the priests of Apollo and various officials from Delphi, Histiaea, and Amphissa.
