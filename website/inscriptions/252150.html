AI Analysis Summary

This is a significant honorific decree from Miletus dating to the Hellenistic period. It honors Eirenias for his successful diplomatic missions to King Eumenes II of Pergamon and King Antiochus (likely Antiochus IV Epiphanes). Eirenias secured a donation of 160,000 medimnoi of grain for the construction of a gymnasium and obtained a tax exemption (ateleia) for Milesian agricultural products imported into the Seleucid kingdom. In recognition, the city awarded him a golden statue to be set up in a prominent place, with the announcement of honors to take place during the Dionysia festival. The text also mentions the role of the brother of King Antiochus in these negotiations.
