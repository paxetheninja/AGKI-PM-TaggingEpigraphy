AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Perge consists of honorific decrees for Stasias, son of Bokos, a prominent citizen who served as a general (strategos) and gymnasiarch. The city (demos and boule) honors him for his virtue (kalokagathia), his role in maintaining peace and safety, and his generous conduct toward the youth (ephebes and neoi) in the gymnasium. The honors include a golden crown and a bronze statue to be erected in the sanctuary of Artemis Pergaia, the city's patron goddess. The text is fragmentary but preserves the essential honorific formulas and the names of the offices held by the honorand.
