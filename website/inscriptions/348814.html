AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a royal ordinance or letter, likely from the Attalid period (165/164 BC), concerning the settlement of Mysian military colonists (katoikoi) in the region of Lydia. It details the surveying of land by Lykon, the granting of tax exemptions (tithes), and the reconstruction of houses destroyed during a previous war. It also establishes religious provisions for Zeus of the Army and the local deity Kournoubeudos, including the appointment of a priest named Bakchios and financial support from the royal treasury for sacrifices.
