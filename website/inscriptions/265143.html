AI Analysis Summary

This extensive inscription from Aphrodisias records the prize money and administrative costs for a major festival. It is divided into musical (mousikos) and athletic (gymnikos) sections. The musical section includes trumpeters, heralds, poets, and various types of flute players and actors. The athletic section covers stadium running, wrestling, boxing, and the pancration, categorized by age groups (boys, youths, men). It also details equestrian events and logistical costs for the stadium, theater, and officials.
