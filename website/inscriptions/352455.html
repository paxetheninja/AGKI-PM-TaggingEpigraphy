AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Greek translation of a Roman Senatus Consultum (135 BC) concerning a long-standing territorial dispute between Priene and Samos over the Karion/Batinetis region. The Roman Senate, presided over by the consul Servius Fulvius Flaccus, confirms the earlier arbitration of the Rhodians. The text is a crucial document for understanding Roman diplomatic intervention in Greek interstate disputes and the legal status of previous arbitrations. It lists the Roman senatorial committee (witnesses) and the diplomatic delegations from both cities.
