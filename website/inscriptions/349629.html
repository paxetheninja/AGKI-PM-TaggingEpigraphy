AI Analysis Summary

This mid-2nd century BC inscription from Halasarna on the island of Kos is an honorific decree for a citizen who served as a 'dikastagogos'. He was sent to Lindos (on Rhodes) to bring back two foreign judges and a secretary to adjudicate legal matters (contracts) in Kos. The text emphasizes his zeal, his proper conduct during festivals, and his personal financial contributions to the mission. It serves as a classic example of inter-city judicial cooperation and the civic honors bestowed upon those who facilitated such diplomatic and legal exchanges.
