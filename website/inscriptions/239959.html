AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic example of a Delphic manumission record, dated to the archonship of Kleon. It documents the fictitious sale of a female slave named Soterycha to the god Apollo Pythios by her owner Sosikrates for the sum of three silver minae. The manumission is conditional upon a 'paramone' agreement, where Soterycha must remain in service to Sosikrates for the remainder of his life. The text includes a list of witnesses, including the priests of Apollo and local officials, and provides legal protections for the manumitted woman against future claims of ownership.
