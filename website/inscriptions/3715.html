AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a fragment of an Athenian inventory of the Treasurers of Athena and the Other Gods (tamiai ton allon theon), dating to the mid-4th century BC. It lists various votive offerings (anathemata) and sacred property kept on the Acropolis or in associated sanctuaries. The text describes the physical state of statues (e.g., 'naked boy', 'missing hands') and identifies objects by their location or the archonship under which they were recorded (Diokles, Pythodoros). It reflects the rigorous administrative oversight of temple treasures in classical Athens.
