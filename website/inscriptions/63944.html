This is an Athenian honorific decree found on Delos, dating to approximately 150 BC during the period of the Athenian cleruchy. It honors Dioscourides of the Athenian deme Myrrhinous and his daughters, Anniche and Theodote, for their hospitality (philanthropia) and piety (eusebeia). The text specifically mentions hospitality provided to visiting Athenians and those arriving on triemioliai (ships). The decree mandates the erection of a stone stele in the sanctuary of Artemis on Delos.
