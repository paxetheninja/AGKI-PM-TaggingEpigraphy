This inscription (ISmyrn 578) is an honorific decree from the city of Smyrna for two judges from Knidos, Xenokritos and Hegesikrates. The text outlines the honors bestowed upon them, including the erection of bronze statues on marble bases in the sanctuary of Aphrodite Stratonikis, gold crowns to be announced at the Dionysia, and the granting of citizenship (politeia). It also contains detailed financial instructions for the treasurer regarding the cost of the crowns and the publication of the decree on a stone stele. The presence of numerous square brackets indicates significant lacunae that have been restored by epigraphists.
