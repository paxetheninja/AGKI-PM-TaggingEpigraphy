AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree from the city of Smyrna for two judges, Xenokritos and Hegesikrates, who were sent from Knidos. The text details the honors bestowed upon them, including gold crowns and a bronze statue to be placed in the sanctuary of Aphrodite Stratonikis. It also describes the public announcement of these honors during the Dionysia festival and provides administrative details for the treasurer to fund the production of the crowns and the stele. The father of Xenokritos is identified as Xenokrates.
