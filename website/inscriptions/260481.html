AI Analysis Summary

This decree concerns the recognition of the asylia (inviolability) of Magnesia on the Maeander and the acceptance of the panhellenic games (Leukophryena) in honor of Artemis Leukophryene. It details the appointment of theoroi (sacred envoys), their travel allowance, and the hospitality (xenia) provided to the Magnesian ambassadors at the Prytaneion.
