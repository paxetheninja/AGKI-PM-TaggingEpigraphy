AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an Athenian ephebic catalogue dating to the archonship of Xenon (133/2 BC). It lists the ephebes who served during that year, organized by their tribes (Erechtheis, Aegeis, Pandionis, Leontis, Ptolemais, and Akamantis). The text identifies the kosmetes, Menekleides son of Theophemos of Kydathenaion, who was the official in charge of the ephebes' training. The document provides significant prosopographical data for the Athenian elite in the late 2nd century BC.
