AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an Athenian decree from 334 BC honoring Peisitheides of Delos. It grants him Athenian citizenship (naturalization) and provides a daily maintenance allowance (trophe) of one drachma. The text outlines the administrative procedures for recording the decree on the Akropolis, the financial responsibilities of the treasurer (tamias), and the involvement of the lawgivers (nomothetai) to ensure the funding is properly allocated. It also includes a protection clause declaring anyone who kills Peisitheides an enemy of the Athenian people.
