This inscription, found on Delos but concerning the cities of Lesbos, is a regulatory document or treaty (suntheke) dating to the early 2nd century BC. It details the administrative and financial reorganization of the Lesbian Koinon (League). The text lists prytaneis from Mytilene, Methymna, Antissa, and Eresos, and outlines provisions for military pay (misthophora), the collection of a 1/60th tax on land/produce, and the harmonization of legal systems through the introduction of common laws (nomoi) and judicial procedures. It reflects the complex inter-city relations and federal structures of the Hellenistic period.
