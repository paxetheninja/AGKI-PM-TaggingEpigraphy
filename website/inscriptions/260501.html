AI Analysis Summary

This inscription (ITral 21) is a Hellenistic decree from the city of Tralles. It records the city's official response to an embassy from Magnesia on the Maeander. The Magnesians had requested that other Greek cities recognize the 'asylia' (inviolability) of their city and territory, and the 'isopythian' status of their newly reorganized festival and games in honor of Artemis Leukophryene, following an oracle from Apollo at Delphi. Tralles here formally accepts these requests, grants honors to the Magnesian ambassadors (Nikodemos and Isagoras), and votes to send its own sacred envoys (theoroi) to participate in the festival and dedicate a silver phiale to the goddess.
