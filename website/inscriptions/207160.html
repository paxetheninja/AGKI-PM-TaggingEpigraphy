AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from Chyretiai in Thessaly dates to the Roman period. It consists of a series of manumission records where individuals pay a fee of 22 denarii and 4 obols to the city upon their release from slavery. The records are organized chronologically by the terms of local officials, specifically the treasurers (tamiai) and the federal strategos of the Thessalian League.
