AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a posthumous honorific decree from the city of Kolophon, found at the Sanctuary of Apollo in Klaros. It honors a citizen named Athenaios by establishing an annual festival in his memory. The decree mandates a sacrifice, a torch race (diadrome) for youths and ephebes, and athletic contests for boys to be held in the Homereion (a building dedicated to Homer) during the month of Kronion. It also details the administrative responsibilities of the gymnasiarch and paidonomos, and the distribution of sacrificial meat and prizes among officials and participants.
