AI Analysis Summary

This inscription (IPri 3) is a significant honorific decree from the city of Priene dating to the late 4th century BC. It honors Megabyzos, the high priest of Artemis at Ephesus, for his goodwill and his contributions toward the completion of the Temple of Athena Polias in Priene. The decree grants him proxeny, the title of benefactor (euergetes), a golden crown, and a bronze statue to be erected in the sanctuary of Athena. It also details specific privileges such as the right to own land (enktesis) and tax exemptions (ateleia), while providing administrative instructions for the announcement of these honors at the Dionysia festival.
