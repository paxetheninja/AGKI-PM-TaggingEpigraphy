AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant historical document from Pagai (ca. 192 BC) recording the resolution of a territorial dispute with neighboring Aigosthena. The process involved the mediation of the Achaean and Boeotian Leagues, who requested neutral judges from the cities of Thyrrheion and Kassope. The decree honors the Achaean League, the city of Sicyon, and the individual judges for their fair arbitration and goodwill, granting them various civic privileges such as proxeny and tax exemptions.
