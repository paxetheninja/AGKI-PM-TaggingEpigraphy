AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a victory list from the Theseia, an Athenian festival honoring Theseus. It records winners in a wide variety of events, including military displays (euandria, euoplia), cavalry exercises, torch races (lampas), and traditional athletic contests like the stadion (sprint), dolichos (long race), and boxing (pygme). The victors are categorized by age groups (boys of different classes, ephebes, young men, and men) and their Athenian tribes. The presence of a Chalcidian victor in the men's sprint indicates the participation of non-Athenians in certain events.
