AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a naval catalogue from Athens, dated to 409 BCE. It lists trierarchs (ship commanders), taxiarchs (infantry commanders), and the 'archon of the navy' (commander of the fleet). The names are grouped under tribal headings (Pandionis, Leontis, Antiochis). This document likely records the officers who served in the Athenian fleet during the Hellespontine campaigns of the Peloponnesian War, possibly following the victory at Cyzicus. The text is heavily fragmented but follows a strict stoichedon pattern typical of late 5th-century Athenian public records.
