AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a manumission record from Delphi, dated to 173 BC during the archonship of Sosinikos. It follows the standard formula of a 'fictitious sale' to Apollo Pythios. Larisa sells her slave Mithradates, a Cappadocian, to the god for 4 minae. The document includes a 'paramone' clause requiring Mithradates to remain with Larisa as long as she lives, performing his duties. It specifies legal protections for his eventual freedom and lists witnesses, including priests of Apollo and local archons. The text is fragmentary, as evidenced by the lacunae and restorations in the provided transcription.
