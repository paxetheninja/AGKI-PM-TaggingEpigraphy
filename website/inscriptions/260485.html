AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a decree from the city of Erythrai (identified via the IMagn corpus context) honoring a judicial commission sent from Magnesia on the Maeander. The commission, consisting of three judges (Philokrates, Sokrates, Aristagoras) and a secretary (Moschos), is praised for resolving legal disputes and restoring civic harmony. The honors granted are extensive: golden crowns, bronze statues, citizenship, and the right of proedria (front-row seating) at games. The decree was to be published on stone stelae in both the sanctuary of Zeus and the agora of Magnesia to ensure the lasting memory of the judges' virtue and the gratitude of the issuing city.
