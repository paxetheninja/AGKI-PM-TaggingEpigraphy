AI Analysis Summary

This text represents the 11th list of the Athenian Tribute Lists (ATL), dating to the archonship of 444/3 BCE. It records the names of the allied cities of the Delian League organized by tribute districts (Ionian, Hellespontine, Thracian, Carian, and Island). The list specifically tracks the 'aparchai' (first-fruits), which was 1/60th of the total tribute (phoros) paid by each city, dedicated to the treasury of Athena Polias. The inscription is in the Attic dialect and stoichedon script, typical of Athenian imperial administration.
