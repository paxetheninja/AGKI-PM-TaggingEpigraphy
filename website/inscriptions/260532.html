AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Hellenistic honorific decree from Magnesia on the Maeander (IMagn 9). It records the granting of proxeny, citizenship (politeia), the right to own property (enktesis), and tax exemptions (ateleia) to foreign benefactors. The text includes a full prescript with the names of the stephanephoros, the presiding tribe, the epistates, and the secretary. It concludes with instructions to erect the stele in the sanctuary of Artemis Leukophryene, the patron deity of the city. The text is fragmentary, with significant lacunae in the names of the honorees and some formulaic sections.
