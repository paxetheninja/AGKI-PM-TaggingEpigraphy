AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant diplomatic document from the Hellenistic period (ca. 182 BC). It records a decree by the Aetolian League accepting the invitation of King Eumenes II of Pergamon to recognize the Nikephoria games as 'stephanitic' (crown-bearing) and equal in status to the Pythian and Olympic games. The decree also grants asylia to the sanctuary of Athena Nikephoros in Pergamon, honors the Attalid royal family with golden statues, and establishes administrative procedures for the games' announcement and the reception of envoys. The text is fragmentary but largely reconstructible through parallel decrees.
