AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a mid-2nd century BC honorific decree from the people of Chalcis for judges sent from Kos. The text details the judges' successful mediation of legal disputes and their virtuous conduct. It prescribes honors such as golden crowns, invitations to the Prytaneion, and public proclamations at the Dionysia, Asklepieia, and Romaia festivals. The decree was to be set up in the Agora and the Sanctuary of Asclepius in Kos.
