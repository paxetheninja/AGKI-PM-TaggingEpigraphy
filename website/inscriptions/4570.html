This inscription is a mid-2nd century BCE subscription list (epidosis) from Attica. It records contributions made by various Athenian citizens, identified by their names and demotics, for the 'kataskeuen tou theatrou' (construction or renovation of the theater). The donors typically contribute 'hyper autou' (on behalf of themselves) and often include their wives and children in the dedication, providing significant prosopographical data for Hellenistic Athens. The text is fragmentary, with several names and demotics partially lost or reconstructed.
