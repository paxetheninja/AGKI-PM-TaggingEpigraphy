AI Analysis Summary

This is a prytany decree from the Athenian Agora dated to the archonship of Hagnotheos (140/39 BC). It honors the prytaneis of the tribe Antiochis for their successful performance of sacrifices to Apollo Prostaterios, Artemis Boulaia, and other traditional deities. The text includes the standard administrative details of the Athenian democracy, such as the names of the secretary, proposer, and various officials (treasurer, herald, flute-player), followed by a register of the prytaneis by deme. The stone is fragmentary, requiring significant restoration of the formulaic sections.
