AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is the 13th annual list of the Athenian Tribute Lists (ATL), dating to 442/1 BCE. It records the 'aparche' (one-sixtieth) of the tribute paid by the member states of the Delian League, which was dedicated to the goddess Athena. The text is organized by geographical districts (Ionian, Hellespontine, Thracian, Carian, and Nesiotic/Insular) and lists the presiding officials, including the secretary Chalcideus of Melite and the Hellenotamias Astyros of Leukonoie. The presence of brackets and missing letters indicates the fragmentary state of the stone.
