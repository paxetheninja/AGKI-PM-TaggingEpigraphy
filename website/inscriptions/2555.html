AI Analysis Summary

This is an Athenian decree from 333/2 BC honoring Pytheas of Alopeke for his diligent work as the superintendent of fountains. He is specifically praised for constructing a new fountain at the sanctuary of Ammon and repairing the fountain at the Amphiaraion. The decree provides details on the Athenian administrative process, including the names of the archon, secretary, and proposer, and specifies the cost and placement of the commemorative stelae.
