AI Analysis Summary

This inscription, known as the 'Great List of Delphic Theorodokoi' (SGDI 2580), is a monumental administrative document from the sanctuary of Apollo at Delphi, dating to approximately 230-220 BC. It was inscribed on the walls of the Treasury of the Cyrenaeans. The text provides a comprehensive list of individuals (theorodokoi) in hundreds of cities across the Mediterranean who were officially designated to host the Delphic envoys (theoroi) announcing the Pythian games. The list is organized by geographical routes, offering a unique snapshot of the Hellenistic world's political and religious networks, including regions as far as Cyprus, Macedonia, Thrace, Crete, North Africa, and the Western Mediterranean (Massalia, Sicily, Italy).
