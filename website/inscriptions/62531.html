AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a formal inventory of the treasures of the Artemision and other temples on Delos, dated to 364/3 BC by the archons Timokrates (Athens) and Aetion (Delos). It records the handover of sacred objects—including phialai, kylikes, crowns, jewelry, and various currencies—from the Athenian Amphictyons to their successors. The text provides critical data for the economic history of Delos, temple administration, and the prosopography of 4th-century BC Athens and the Cyclades.
