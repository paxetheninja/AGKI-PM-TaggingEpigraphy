AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree from the city of Oropos, dating to the mid-2nd century BC. It records the city's gratitude towards foreign judges and ambassadors sent from Thebes and Eretria. These judges were requested to settle legal disputes (dikazein) according to a pre-existing treaty (symbolon). The text emphasizes the judges' impartiality and their role in maintaining 'syngeneia' (kinship) and 'philia' (friendship) between the cities. The decree grants them golden crowns and other honors, and orders the publication of the decree in the sanctuary of Amphiaraos (Amphiareion).
