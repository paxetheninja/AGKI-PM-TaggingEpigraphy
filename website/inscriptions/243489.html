AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a classic example of an agonistic victory list from the Roman Imperial period (dated to 214 AD). It documents the career of Aurelius Septimius Eutychus, a boxer and pancratiast from Laodicea. The text is highly structured, moving from his titles and major 'sacred' games (Pythian, Actian, Olympian) to a long list of 'talent-prize' (talantiaioi) games in various cities across Syria, Phoenicia, and Cilicia. It concludes with a precise date using both Roman consular dating and local eponymous officials.
