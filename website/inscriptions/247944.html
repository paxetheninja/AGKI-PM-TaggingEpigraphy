AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an imperial rescript from the joint reign of Marcus Aurelius and Lucius Verus (dated between 161-169 AD). It is addressed to Ulpius Eurycles, who served as the logistes (financial auditor) of the Gerousia of Ephesus. The document is a crucial source for understanding Roman imperial intervention in local civic finances and the management of the imperial cult, specifically regarding the maintenance of silver statues and the recovery of outstanding debts owed to the council of elders.
