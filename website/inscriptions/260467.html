AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Hellenistic decree from the city of Epidamnos, found at Magnesia on the Maeander. It is part of the 'Asylia dossier' of Magnesia, a collection of decrees from various Greek states recognizing the city's status as sacred and inviolable (asylia) following an epiphany of Artemis Leukophryene. The text details the arrival of Magnesian ambassadors (Sosikles, Aristodamos, and Diotimos), their presentation of an oracle from Delphi, and their historical merits, including the defense of Delphi against 'barbarians' (Gauls) and their mediation in a civil war in Crete. The Epidamnians agree to recognize the asylia and the panhellenic status of the Leukophryena games, granting the ambassadors proxeny and other honors.
