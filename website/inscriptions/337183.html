AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is one of the 'Entella Tablets', a series of bronze documents from the mid-3rd century BC. It records a decree by the city of Entella expressing gratitude to several other Sicilian communities (the Petrinoi, Kytattarinoi, Mischeron, and Makellon) and specific individuals for providing grain during a period of synoikismos (resettlement or political unification). The text grants these benefactors proxenia and isopoliteia, and lists the specific quantities of wheat (spyroi) and barley (krithai) contributed. The historical context is likely the First Punic War, during which Entella faced significant displacement and food shortages.
