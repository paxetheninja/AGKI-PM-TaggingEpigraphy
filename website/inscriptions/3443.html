This inscription is a decree of the Salaminians dated to the archonship of Sarapion (116/5 BC). It honors three supervisors (Theodotos, Aischron, and Charikles) for their oversight of the repair of sanctuaries and the construction of an exedra. The text provides administrative details regarding the publication of the decree on a stone stele, the funding of the monument, and the inclusion of a list of donors. The presence of demotics (Piraieus, Aixoneus, Leukonoeus) confirms the Athenian context of the Salaminian administration during this period.
