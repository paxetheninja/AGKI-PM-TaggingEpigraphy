AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a Hellenistic decree from the city of Syracuse, found at Magnesia on the Maeander. It belongs to a large archive of similar decrees from various Greek cities recognizing the 'asylia' (inviolability) of Magnesia and the panhellenic status of the festival of Artemis Leukophryene. The text mentions the arrival of Magnesian ambassadors and sacred envoys (theoroi), the recognition of the goddess's epiphany, and the formal acceptance of the games as 'isopythian' (equal to the Pythian games in prestige and prize-type). It also confirms the status of the city and its territory as sacred and inviolable according to an oracle.
