AI Analysis Summary

This inscription from the Asklepieion of Kos is a significant subscription list (epidosis) dating to approximately 242 BC. It records voluntary contributions from citizens and foreigners (including individuals from Alexandria, Kalymnos, and Priene) to fund the gymnic games or sanctuary maintenance. The text follows a standard decree format followed by a catalog of names and amounts in drachmae, ranging from small sums to 1000 drachmae.
