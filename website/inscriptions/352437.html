AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is an honorific decree from the late 3rd century BC, found in Priene but concerning relations with Colophon. It honors an individual (likely Artemon, based on restorations) for his services as an ambassador. The text details the publication of the decree in two major religious centers: the sanctuary of Apollo at Klaros (near Colophon) and the sanctuary of Athena Polias in Priene. It also mentions the announcement of the honors during major festivals including the Dionysia, the Klaria, and the Antiocheia, reflecting the interconnectedness of Ionian poleis and the importance of public recognition in agonistic and religious contexts.
