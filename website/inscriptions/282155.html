AI Analysis Summary

This 3rd-century AD inscription from Pisidia records a collective dedication by the 'Xenoi Tekmoreioi', a religious association. The members funded the creation of a statue (andreias), a bronze vessel (chalkoma), a phiale, and an incense burner (libanotris) for the goddess Artemis and the Tyche of the Emperor. The text is primarily composed of a long list of members, many bearing the nomen Aurelius, indicating the period following the Constitutio Antoniniana.
