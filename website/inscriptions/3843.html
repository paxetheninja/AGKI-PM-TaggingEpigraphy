This inscription is part of the Athenian Naval Catalogues (tabulae curatorum navalium), specifically dating to the mid-4th century BC (Archonship of Kallimachos, 349/8 BC). It records the inventory of equipment for various triremes (named ships like Nikesis, Nereis, Parthenos, Aphrodisias, and Kekropis) stored in the Piraeus arsenal (skeuotheke) and ship-sheds (neosoikoi). The text lists specific items such as oars (tarros), rudders (pedalia), ladders (klimakides), and masts (histos), noting whether they are 'dokimos' (fit for service).
