AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a mid-2nd century BC decree from the city of Apollonia ad Rhyndacum, found in Miletos. It records a diplomatic mission sent to Miletos to renew the ancestral kinship (syngeneia) between the two cities. The text provides a mythological and historical justification for this link, claiming Apollonia was a colony founded by Miletos during military campaigns in the Hellespont and Propontis regions, led by the guidance of Apollo Didymeus. The decree honors the Milesians for acknowledging these historical records and for their hospitality toward the Apollonian ambassadors.
