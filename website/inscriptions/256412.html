AI Analysis Summary

This inscription is a significant Hellenistic decree from Teos concerning the 'Koinon of the Dionysiac Artists' (technitai). It details the purchase of land (ktema) for the association, the financial administration involving city treasurers (tamiai), and the appointment of commissioners to oversee the property. The text highlights the close relationship between the city and the guild, providing for the decree to be displayed both in the sanctuary of Dionysos and on the theater's parastas. It mentions the prytanis Metrodoros, serving as a chronological marker for the city's administration.
